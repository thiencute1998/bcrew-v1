    HOW IT WORKS

    STEP 1:

    Please send us the samples (03 outputs) for free trial.
    Dropbox, Wetransfer, Google Driver or any way is available to us (share the link via the mail: info.bcrew@gmail.com).

    STEP 2:

    We will create and share to you the Dropbox folder. Please upload your original files to that.

    STEP 3:

    Email to us to confirm the task, and write your instruction as well.

    STEP 4:

    The Bcrew team will process and take the Q/C process for 2 rounds to ensure the quality of product.

    STEP 5:

    We will upload to your Dropbox folder, and let you know once your order is completed.

    STEP 6:

    For regular orders, our invoice will be sent to you at the end of month. Payments can be made via Paypal, Western Union, or Bank transfer.
    Note: If you pay via Paypal, you will be charged an additional 5% due to the regulations of Paypal in our country.
